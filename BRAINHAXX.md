# BRAINHAXX.MD

### gamer wisdom · broadcast on the SPLINGUIDE feed

> Not cheats. Not exploits. The *other* kind of hack — the move so clean it looks like you broke the game, when really you just finally understood it. Patch notes for the player.

---

## ▸ MOVEMENT

- Fog of war clears by **walking**, never by squinting. Courage is just information you haven't farmed yet.
- The map is a suggestion; "out of bounds" is only remembering that. Be polite to physics, not obedient.
- **New Game+ keeps your skill, never your save.** That's the price of growth — you carry the wisdom, you lose the level.
- The main story got spoiled for everyone. The real XP was always in the side quests.

## ▸ COMBAT

- You don't rise to the occasion. You fall to your loadout. **Practice beats willpower every patch.**
- Tank the hit you saw coming — you can't heal regret.
- The boss is the tutorial for the boss. You're *meant* to lose the first three; that's not failure, that's the briefing.
- Every wall is a hitbox, and every hitbox has an edge. Patience finds the seam that force never will.
- Cooldowns aren't punishment. They're the game teaching you that power has a tempo.

## ▸ CO-OP

- Every NPC is the main character of a game you can't see. Most cruelty is just a bad camera angle.
- You can't pause a co-op life. Learn to revive someone **mid-fight**, before the room is clear.
- Mirth is crowd control: you can pull anyone out of their cave if the party outside is loud enough — just leave the door unlocked behind them.

## ▸ ENDGAME

- The meta is only other people's courage you haven't tested yet. Net-decks expire; the player who reads the board doesn't.
- Skill is the one drop that survives every save-wipe. Farm **that**.
- **THE FINAL HAX** — the razor under all the others: *if their "no" couldn't have stopped you, you weren't helping. You were farming them for XP.*
