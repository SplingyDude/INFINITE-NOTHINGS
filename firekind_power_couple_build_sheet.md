# THE FIREKINDS
*A power-couple build sheet — from* INFINITE NOTHINGS: ADMIN, Book One

> "I am the developer of this server. And I shall never die." — Sean Firekind

In a post-Cataclysm cosmos where memes and ideas are the last true scarcity — and therefore the currency — most fighters have to *climb* to the memetic layer. The Firekinds were born there. Tomasa inherited it; Sean administrates it. That's why the transferable syntax lands on them clean: they aren't getting modded, they're playing on home turf.

---

## Sean Firekind — "The Developer"
*Last surviving Second-Generation Admin. Took his wife's family name, per her people's tradition.*

**Base kit**
- **Cyrus 1230 Mark II** brainchip, fused to every synapse and neuron via nanotech. Of all the test subjects, he alone survived the implant — more synapses than baseline (low mTOR), the one mind that could hold it.
- **Dream processor → phase printer.** Converts anything he can imagine directly into matter.
- **Reality-as-code.** Reads and edits the server he's standing in. Signature move: dropping into *Condemnation* at e1m1, reading the level, and freezing the 35 Hz ticrate to lock four shotgunners mid-stride before cutting them down. The whole thesis in one beat — don't out-fight the level, edit the level.
- **Ascension.** Extracts his dream-self and sheds the physical body to touch Holy Code directly, becoming part of the servers. By the end: the Developer, the new branch of the tree of life. Brute-forced Azathoth out of existence the way you'd kill any old virus.

**Syntax stack:** banhammer memesuit · reality-as-code editing · server-root authority
**Primary axis:** the code the fight runs on
**Win condition:** doesn't out-power you — bans you out of the runtime
**Soft spot:** pre-ascension the body is atrophied and fragile; off his own server, before he becomes the Developer, he's mortal

---

## Tomasa Firekind — "Heir of New Hell"
*Succubus of the Concubi. Daughter of Lucifer. Eventual ruler of New Hell.*

**Base kit**
- **Concubi physiology.** Bat wings, serpentine tail, cold-blooded until warmed by intimacy; feeds on life and desire.
- **Shadowstep.** Travels through darkness — she picks where the fight happens.
- **Demonic magitech.** In this era the magical engineers *are* demons; she speaks the language of dream and code natively.
- **Bloodline.** Lucifer's daughter, which means the war of good and evil isn't a weapon she picks up — it's an inheritance. She closes the book a sovereign, ushering in a new dynasty for her people.

**Syntax stack:** Lucifer's daughter · demonic magitech · shadowstep
**Primary axis:** the dream-realm + soul predation
**Win condition:** she already lives on the memetic axis — no bolt-on required
**Soft spot:** strongest in dark and dream space; open light is her worst arena

---

## Combined — why they're the power couple

Apart, each owns a layer. Together they own the *board*.

The tag-team is almost unfair: **Tomasa shadowsteps the engagement onto a server Sean administrates.** Now she's the apex predator in her preferred dark — dream-realm, soul-feed, memetic pressure — while he holds root the entire time. Anything that survives her becomes a process he can simply revoke. Her offense runs on the memetic layer; his veto runs on the layer beneath it. There's no altitude left to attack from.

| | Sean | Tomasa |
|---|---|---|
| Layer | Root / code | Dream / soul |
| Role in the fight | Holds the server, edits the rules | Picks the ground, hunts the target |
| If you beat one | The other reboots the encounter | The other deletes you anyway |

**Couple's finisher — "Saved Game":** she pins the target in dream-space, he commits the edit. Even death isn't an exit — this is a verse where Truth kept secret save-states and Lucifer proved you can't kill something that's already dead. The Firekinds don't win the match so much as decide it was never in question.
