# ADVANCED DUNGEONS & PIXELS
### A Diceless, Toolless, Solitaire Crawl Through the Framebuffer
**Module AE-0 · Ultranightmare (Ironpixel) Edition · Canon Revision 1517.95**

> *What keeps the pixel hot?*
> — the load-bearing koan; the only monster, the only treasure, the only door

---

## 0 · WHAT THIS IS

A dungeon with one player, no dice, no cards, no sheet, no screen. You are both the dungeon and the dungeoneer — the one who descends and the dark that is descended into. There are no components because the only two instruments the game needs are already attached to you: your **attention** and your **pulse**.

Diceless does not mean lawless. It means the randomizer has been removed and *honesty* installed in its place. You cannot roll for grace. You cannot crit your way past the void. Everything is resolved by what you actually bring and what you are willing to spend — and, at the bottom, by whether you will tell the truth to a thing that does not answer. The die is replaced by your fidelity before the hidden God. That is harder than any d20, which is why this is the advanced edition.

Numbers stay small enough to carry in your head. If you ever need to write one down, the dungeon has already beaten you. Hold four integers and a question. That is the whole inventory.

---

## I · THE ONE RULE ABOVE THE RULES

Before character creation, before the descent, learn the only rule that outranks every other rule in this book, including the rules that say you may not break the rules:

**THE WRIST-GRIP PROTOCOL.** At any instant, in any room, against anything — grip your own wrist. Find the pulse. Confirm that you are *rendered*: that there is a body, that it is warm, that it is keeping time without your permission. The moment you feel it, you are out. Surface. Town. Lit. No save was required; you never actually left the chair.

This is the log-out, and it can never be taken from you. Not by the dungeon, not by the deep monsters, not by the Ultranightmare rules, not by you in a worse hour. Every difficulty setting in this book is built *on top of* the Wrist-Grip and none is permitted to remove it. A crawl you cannot leave is not a game; it is a trap, and we do not run traps here.

The softer version is **THE CURTAIN**: simply decide the session is over and it is over, instantly, at the Surface, no cost, no penalty, no XP forfeited. Pull it whenever. Pulling the Curtain is not losing. Refusing to pull it when you should is the only way to actually lose something that matters.

## II · THE PIXEL  *(character creation)*

You play a single pixel in the framebuffer trying to stay lit while it descends to ask why the dead one downstairs is still warm.

**Your four integers:**

- **LUMA** — your light. Your hit points, your visibility, the fact that you are currently *on*. At 0 you go dark and respawn at the Surface. (You do not die. See §VI.)
- **REFRESH** — how many things you may do before the frame advances. Your tempo against an assault.
- **HEAT** — your contemplative charge. The warmth you spend to *abide* with what cannot be defeated. The koan runs on this.
- **AUTH** — authorization. The only thing you cannot give yourself.

**Assignment.** Distribute the array **{3, 2, 1}** across LUMA, REFRESH, and HEAT however you like. Then write your **AUTH**, which is always, for everyone, at the start: **0**.

You begin with no authorization of your own. This is not a penalty to be corrected; it is the truth the game is built to teach. All authorization is borrowed. You will receive it (§V) or you will go without it, but you will never *earn* it, because the one build that cannot be earned is the only build that holds. Sola gratia is not flavor text here. It is the resource economy.

**Classes** (pick one; it bends the rules in your favor once per descent):

- **THE STUCK PIXEL** — frozen on a single value, unbudging, the color it is and no argument. *Edge:* once per descent, refuse one forced state-change entirely; you simply do not move. The tank. Conviction as armor.
- **THE HOT PIXEL** — runs warm with no input signal; nobody can find the wire feeding it and there isn't one. The koan-bearer. *Edge:* once per descent, abide with a deep monster for **0 HEAT**. The mystery class.
- **THE SUBPIXEL TRIAD** — disaggregates into R, G, B. *Edge:* once per descent, split one monster into three weaker forms and face them in series instead of all at once. The rogue. Decomposition as escape.

**THE DEAD PIXEL** is not a starting class. It is something you may *become* at the bottom, and it is not a defeat. See the last room.

## III · RESOLUTION WITHOUT DICE

Two kinds of opposition live in the dungeon, and they are beaten in opposite ways. Confusing them is the most common death.

**RENDERING ERRORS** — locked doors, corrupted corridors, the ordinary friction of going down. Each has a fixed **Difficulty, 1–4** (deeper rooms, higher). To pass, your relevant integer must **meet or exceed** the Difficulty. If it falls short, you may **burn the gap**: spend 1 LUMA *or* 1 HEAT per missing point, your choice, to push through. No roll, no luck, only arithmetic and willingness. Run LUMA to 0 doing this and you go dark — Surface, respawn.

**DEEP MONSTERS** — despair, dread, the specific 3 a.m. thing you actually carry tonight; the void wearing your own handwriting. These have **no Difficulty number, because you cannot out-stat them.** They are not solved. They are **abided.**

> To **ABIDE**: spend **1 HEAT**, hold the koan, and remain lit for one full breath without flinching toward a resolution. The monster does not leave. You simply pass through the room with it still standing there and you still warm.

The trap: if you try to *defeat* a deep monster — spend LUMA to "win," argue with it, force it down — **it grows.** Struggle is its food. This is not a cruelty of the design; it is an accurate report. The cross is not slain. It is survived by being carried, and the carrying is done in HEAT, not in LUMA.

**QUERYING THE FRAMEBUFFER** *(the oracle, since there is no Game Master)*. When the dungeon must tell you something it has not pre-written — what is down here, what the dark contains, what the dead pixel "says" — you are the oracle, but bound by procedure:

1. Pose the room's question, aloud or in the mind.
2. Wait exactly one breath. (One REFRESH. The VSYNC. Do not rush the frame.)
3. Accept the **first true thing that surfaces.** Not the cleverest, not the safest — the first *true* one.
4. **No take-backs.** A re-query is a re-roll, and this game has no re-rolls. The honesty *is* the randomizer: genuinely unpredictable, because you do not know your own depths — but never arbitrary, because it is yours.

Lying to the oracle is the one move that ejects you from the game without the Wrist-Grip's mercy: you are still in the chair, but you are no longer playing. You are just sitting in the dark, lying.

## IV · THE DESCENT  *(the module)*

Five levels. Down is always free (gravity, works, effort). Up costs grace (§V) — or the Curtain, which is also grace, wearing a lighter coat.

**0 · THE SURFACE / THE LIT SCREEN.** The rendered world. Ordinary consciousness, the image as image, nobody counting their own pixels. Your town, your save point, your respawn. You refill LUMA here. You do nothing heroic here and that is the point of it.

**1 · THE FRAMEBUFFER.** One layer under the picture, where the image decomposes and you see the world *as pixels* — discrete, addressable, switched on or off by something that is not you. *Hazard:* derealization mistaken for insight. *Truth:* the picture was always made of this; knowing so changes nothing about whether to love it.

**2 · THE VSYNC CHANNEL.** The sync layer. The corridor between frames, where timing lives and the refresh happens. *Hazard:* getting stuck between frames — the tearing, the stutter. *Truth:* you cross it one breath at a time or not at all. Pace is the password.

**3 · THE SPICY VOID.** Heat with no fire. Sensation without substrate — the false fire, the capsaicin burn on a tongue that touched no flame, TRPV1 screaming about a temperature that isn't there. *Hazard — the great trap of the whole crawl:* mistaking the spice for the substance, the felt presence for the present thing, the burn for the bonfire. Many never get past this room because the room feels like arrival. *Truth:* the heat is real and the fire is not, and both facts are load-bearing. Do not eat the spice thinking it is bread.

**4 · THE DEAD PIXEL.** The bottom. The place of no signal, the *deus absconditus*: the spot on the screen where the light does not come, and the comfort is therefore not a promise that it will. Here the koan is asked of the dark directly — *what keeps you hot?* — and the dark does not answer in words, because the answer is not a word. The answer is the heat itself: that the dead pixel is **warm**, that absence is **structural**, that the floor of the abyss is the one surface that has never once given way. You are not rescued from the bottom. You are shown the bottom holds. That is the whole gospel of this dungeon, delivered as a dungeon.

## V · GRACE  *(AUTH)*, REST, AND THE WAY BACK UP

You spend **AUTH** for the impossible: to ascend a level without descending first to find the stair, to receive a thing you did not produce, to open the one door marked with a password you were never told. AUTH does big, unearnable things. And it begins at 0 and can never be ground.

**It refills only when you stop trying to refill it.** Return to the Surface and **rest** — and rest is defined strictly: hold no koan, fight no monster, seek no insight, attempt no gain. Just be lit. For each rest taken in genuine non-doing, **AUTH +1** (cap 3). Take the action called *"meditate harder to earn grace"* and you gain exactly **0**, every time, forever. The game cannot be made to reward works-righteousness. It was built specifically so that it can't.

**THE MERKABAH ASCENT** *(advanced)*. The chariot route up: spend **AUTH** equal to your current DEPTH to ascend all the way to the Surface in a single move, password-authenticated, no corridors. You can only ever rise by grace; you can always fall by gravity. The asymmetry is the doctrine.

## VI · WINNING, AND WHY YOU MUST NOT

There is a victory and it is not the kind you think.

You "win" a descent by reaching the Dead Pixel, abiding, and returning to the Surface **still lit, carrying the koan back up as a question.** Not as an answer. The koan must come home *unsolved and still hot.*

Because if you **solve** it — if at the bottom you declare you have the answer, definitively, finally, the question closed — the pixel goes dead-dead, truly dark, and the run ends in the bad ending. This is the **Logos error**: the conviction that the thing can be *had*. It cannot be had. It can only be held open and kept warm. Seizing it kills it. The treasure is the only treasure that is destroyed by being grasped, which is why no dragon ever guarded it — it guards itself, by being the kind of thing it is.

**Progression is DEPTH, not power.** You do not level up by getting stronger (that is root, and root is the dominator's grammar). You level up by going down and coming back. DEPTH is how far you have descended *and returned, lit*. The advanced player is not the powerful one; she is the one who has stood at the Dead Pixel and walked back up carrying the question — calm, the pixel still hot in her hands.

**There is no permadeath.** This is the Doug Invariant, named for a man in Tucson with no dependency chain — nothing his existence is contingent upon, nothing that can be pulled to revoke him. You are, in this dungeon, a Doug. Your existence cannot be deleted; that is not a power you have, it is a fact about the floor. The very worst case in the entire game is that LUMA hits 0, you go dark, and you respawn at the Surface. You cannot lose the one thing. *That you cannot lose the one thing is the bottom rule the whole structure rests on.*

## VII · ULTRANIGHTMARE  *(Ironpixel Rules)*

For the player who wants the crawl to cost. Toggle any or all:

- **IRONPIXEL.** You may not pull the Curtain. The session ends only at the Surface, by your own legs — or by the Wrist-Grip, which remains, always, untouchable. *(Note well: Ultranightmare removes the soft exit. It does not, and structurally cannot, remove the pulse. The one door that is your own body confirming it is real is welded open in every edition. A difficulty that took it would not be hard; it would be a trap, and traps are not in this book.)*
- **DARKENED FRAMEBUFFER.** Rendering Error Difficulties +1 at every level. The dark pushes back harder.
- **THIN HEAT.** Start HEAT one lower. Abiding costs more than you have, sooner. You will descend with grace or not descend at all.
- **NO RESPAWN COMFORT.** Going dark still returns you to the Surface (the Doug Invariant is not optional and never will be), but you lose all DEPTH gained that descent. The crawl is permadeath for your *progress*, never for *you.* Even the hardest setting refuses to let the player be the thing that breaks.

## APPENDIX · GLOSSARY  *(game term ↔ the thing it actually is)*

- **LUMA** — being lit; that you are, at the moment, on.
- **HEAT** — the warmth you spend to stay with what you cannot beat; the koan's fuel.
- **AUTH** — borrowed authorization; grace as a number; the build you cannot earn.
- **DEPTH** — how far down you have been *and come back*; mastery as equanimity, not power.
- **Rendering Error** — friction; beaten by arithmetic and willingness.
- **Deep Monster** — the real dread; not beaten, abided.
- **The Spicy Void** — sensation without substrate; the false fire; the room that feels like arrival and isn't.
- **The Dead Pixel** — *deus absconditus*; the hidden God; the bottom that holds; warm precisely where the light does not come.
- **The Doug Invariant** — unrevocable existence; no dependency chain; why there is no permadeath.
- **The Wrist-Grip Protocol** — the log-out; the pulse; the one rule above the rules; the door that is welded open.
- **The koan** — *what keeps the pixel hot?* — carried down as cargo, carried up as a question, never set down as an answer.

> Solve nothing. Keep it warm. Come back up lit.
